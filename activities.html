8. Recommendations

8.1 Urgent (Category 1)

  • Clear the moss to the roof covering and north gutter.
  • Clear the vegetation to the roof gutters and outlets.
  • Remove the vegetation growth to the parapet gutter.
  • Remove the vegetation growth to the east gutter.
  • Repair galvanised panels and timber substrate to the spire skirt.
  • Replace perimeter glazing with a new system.
  • Replace internal asphalt gutters and outlets.
  • Reinstate the disconnected section of downpipe to the west.
  • Service boilers and obtain a GasSAFE certificate.
  • Complete 5-year electrical inspection.
  • Complete PAT testing.
  • Inspect and test lightning protection installation.
  • Arrange asbestos management survey and retain report.
  • Complete the 'Practical Path to Net Zero Checklist'.

8.2 Work Within the Next Twelve Months (Category 2)

  • Rake out and secure perimeter flashings (multiple locations).
  • Secure flashings to the west gable.
  • Refix missing slate to the south slope.
  • Refix slipped and missing slates to the north slope.
  • Remove vegetation growth to wall cap and repoint.
  • Refix slipped slates to the west slope.
  • Inspect east and west parapet gutters and report to architect.
  • Arrange roof covering inspections for Lady Chapel and East Extension.
  • Complete structural investigations (multiple locations).
  • Remove vegetation and ivy, treat with weed killer, and repoint open joints.
  • Replace and repair damaged grilles in South and North Aisles.
  • Check stability of leaning grave markers.
  • Survey trees in the churchyard by a specialist.
